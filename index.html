Dennis L. Sun

I am an Associate Professor of Statistics at Stanford University and a Senior Data Scientist at Google, Inc.

Until 2022, I was an Associate Professor of Statistics and Affiliate Professor of Computer Science at Cal Poly, San Luis Obispo. I spent Fall 2022 visiting National Taiwan University in Taipei.

My research interests include data science education, statistical software, audio signal processing, and public health. My research has been supported by DARPA, NIH, and the NSF.
